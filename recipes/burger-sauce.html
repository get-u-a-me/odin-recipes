Burger Sauce

This burger sauce has a zippy flavor that goes well in sandwiches or as a fry dip.
It's a tangy sauce that can be made spicy or milder for kids.

  • Condiments
  • Seasonings
  • Hot sauce
  • Vinegar
  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk together mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, onion, garlic, and vinegar in a bowl.
  3. Season with hot sauce and seasoned pepper.
  4. Cover and chill at least 1 hour before serving.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
